How it works

Six detection signals

The engine runs six heuristic checks simultaneously to score AI probability.

01
Sentence length
AI tends to write 14–26 word sentences with suspicious consistency. Human writing varies.
02
Vocabulary diversity
AI reuses fewer unique words per paragraph. High diversity ratio is a strong AI signal.
03
AI phrase detection
Words like "furthermore", "utilize", "leverage", "paramount" and "delve" are AI favorites.
04
Structural variance
AI produces uniformly structured paragraphs. Low variance in sentence length signals AI origin.
05
Transition overuse
AI relies heavily on transition words: "first", "in addition", "as a result", "therefore".
06
Punctuation density
AI overuses commas, semicolons, and colons — higher density than typical human writing.
